About us

Where do BAGS come from?

Once upon a time... a poor bastard had a hobby: video games. Because of his hobby, he came up with the idea of making a game about his misfortune and selling it to people going through the same things as him. To do so, he contacted other similar idiots. Even though none of them could quit their current jobs and didn't have much experience making games, they decided to join him. And so, we created, well..., almost... a video game studio.
