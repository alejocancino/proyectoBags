Barely a Game Studio

Hi! We're BAGS (Barely A Game Studio) and an experimental project of five Argentinians -self-called- creatives. Our goal is to build a decent game without dying of burnout in the process.

Games

L.O.R.A

LORA is a platformer roguelike with Survival logic and some Life-Simulation elements. It is a contemporary fantasy world based on magic instead of technology.

About us

We are a group of poor bastards who are passionate about video games and challenges. That's why we decided to create a video game studio, well barely... a video game studio.
