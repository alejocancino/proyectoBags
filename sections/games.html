Life of a Regular Adventurer

The main aim of the game is to make the hero survive day-to-day life in this modern magical society. To do so, the player must balance the character's life between the adventurer's work, leisure, and social life, maintaining his mental health in the process. Keeping anxiety, depression, and stress at bay while maintaining his economic “independence” will be the real challenge.

The protagonist is a wannabe hero who grew up listening to legends about adventure, glories, and epic tales, and dreamed all his life of living up to those standards. Worse, he was told that, if he dreamed hard enough, tried hard enough, or were as talented as the heroes of those tales, he would be lord and master of his destiny. But adulthood came and, with it, the certainty that the stories omitted... some details... ...the life of a regular adventurer isn't as epic and great as it was told.
